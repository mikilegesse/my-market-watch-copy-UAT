üìä Understanding Market Colors & Terms
üü¢ GREEN = BUYING (Demand)
When someone BUYS USDT or posts a BUY REQUEST.
Indicates demand for USDT, potential capital flight from ETB to crypto assets.
üî¥ RED = SELLING (Supply)
When someone SELLS USDT or posts a SELL REQUEST.
Indicates supply of USDT, capital returning from crypto to ETB.
Key Trading Terms:
Aggressor (Taker)
The person who takes liquidity by filling someone else's ad. Their action (buy or sell) determines the color shown in the feed.
Maker
The person who provides liquidity by posting an ad and waiting for it to be filled. We track aggressor actions, not maker inventory changes.
This approach matches standard exchange behavior and accurately reflects market sentiment.
